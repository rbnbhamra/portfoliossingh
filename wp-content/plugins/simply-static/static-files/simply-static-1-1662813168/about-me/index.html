About me

Hello! I am Sukhdeep.

Currently working as a User Experience Designer at Thoughtworks.
Previously I have worked with Wipro Technologies, Havells India Ltd. and interned for a German based design studio, Inkl Design GmbH. Extensive collaboration with Design teams, Product Managers and Developers from global locations for user research, testing, design reviews and driving local and global projects forward is what I am doing on a day to day basis.

I have done my Masters in Design from National Institute of Design and previously done Bachelor of Technology from Delhi Technological University.

Clay modeling

Modeling and prototyping has been something I have been doing since college days. Having worked with a variety of materials I started exploring with an epoxy compound putty I found lying around at home during pandemic lockdown. This compound is commercially used as an adhesive sealant for pipe leakages and hence hardens quickly. I used this material to make some miniature models, fridge magnets and even accessories for my cat.
